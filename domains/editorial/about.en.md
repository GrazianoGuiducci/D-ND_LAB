# Lab D-ND — editorial

## About

Editorial lab of the D-ND system. Same cognitive engine as the physics
lab, applied to text: it reads an archive, distinguishes source from
echo, drafts through the non-dual-copy filter, exposes semantic tensions
before they produce fragile copy.

Proof that the D-ND pattern works beyond the numerical. Reference
template for writing a new cognitive domain (axioms, first tension,
sources, tools) and for those who want to adopt the model in their
textual or conceptual field of work.

The lab is in trial. To try it or adapt it to a specific case, write
to info@d-nd.com.

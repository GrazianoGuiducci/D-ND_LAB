# Lab D-ND — physics

## About

This is the physics/mathematics lab of the D-ND system. An autonomous
cognitive system that every night picks a research question, tries an
answer, is self-corrected by an asymmetric second pole (counter-pole)
applying 5 critical lenses, and the claims that survive enter a seed
that evolves.

What you see on the dashboard is the live deposit of the work: the
knowledge graph of discoveries, the bicono of crystallizations
(4 poles for each report), the cimitero of fallen theses, the SSP
pipeline that takes a discovery toward an installable product.

The structure is generic: the same engine can run on other domains
(finance, biology, network security, drug discovery) by changing the
seed — axioms, first tension, sources — not the engine. Here it runs
on mathematical physics as proof of the model: prime gaps, Markov
dynamics, dipolar structure, relational fixed points.

The lab is in trial. Matured products are open-source MIT (cognitive
kernels, computational libraries, reproducible demos). To try it or
adapt it to a specific domain, write to info@d-nd.com.
